🏆 Partner Benefits by PVI Level
Each milestone unlocks cumulative benefits. Higher tiers include all lower-tier benefits plus additional advantages.
0.0+
Cisco New Partner
Entry level
  • Cisco Partner status
  • PXP (Partner Experience Platform) access
  • Black Belt Academy training
  • Cisco Commerce platform
  • dCloud demo environment
  • Fast Track Pricing
1.0+
Cisco Basic Partner
All New benefits, plus:
  • Deal Registration — Hunting
  • Deal Registration — Teaming
  • Not-for-Resale (NFR) product access
Deal Registration protects partner-led opportunities and provides margin protection.
5.0+
Cisco Portfolio Partner
All Basic benefits, plus:
  • Cisco (Portfolio) Partner status badge
  • Partner Incentive Program (rebates)
  • Customer Assessment Incentive rewards
Rebate programs provide back-end margin that significantly improves partner profitability.
7.5+
Cisco Preferred Partner
All Portfolio benefits, plus:
  • Cisco Preferred (Portfolio) Partner status badge
  • Higher rebate rates
  • Strategic offer differentiation
  • 10 Cisco U licenses per portfolio
  • Next-Gen Specializations eligibility
Preferred status provides the strongest competitive differentiation and Cisco's highest-value partner programs.
